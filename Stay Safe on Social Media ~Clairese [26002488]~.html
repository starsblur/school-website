The use of strong & unique passwords

Choose long, complex passwords that are unique for each account. Avoid using easily guessable info like birthdays or common words. A password manager can help you create and store strong passwords securely. This lowers the risk of hackers accessing your accounts through weak or reused passwords.

  • Dashlane

Check Your Privacy Settings

Regularly check and update your social media privacy settings to control who can see your posts and personal information. Limit what strangers can view by making your profile private or restricting certain details to friends only. This reduces the risk of unwanted attention, data scraping, or misuse of your personal info.


Stay Alert for Suspicious Links

Scammers often use links and attachments to spread malware or steal your personal information through phishing attacks. Be wary of unexpected messages or DMs even if they seem to come from friends and avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links. When in doubt, verify with the sender before opening anything. You can also check if a link is safe by pasting it into a trusted link-scanning website, like NordVPN checker or Google Safe Browsing.

Be Aware of Impersonators
